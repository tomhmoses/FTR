What? A Winter Skills and Safety Lecture

When? 19:30 On Tuesday the 17th of November

Where? In the Guild Council Chambers in the Guild

How Much? The Bargain Price of Â£3.50

On the 17th of November we are hosting a Winter skills and safety lecture for the British Mountaineering Council. This is aimed at anyone who is interested in spending time in the mountains in winter be it walking, climbing or mountaineering. It will be given by to two Mountain guides and will be packed with inspirational stories and practical advice. And if that's not enough there will be a Free Prize draw for all those who attend! It's also perfect if you're thinking of coming on our winter mountaineering trip to Scotland this year.

More information can be found here: https://www.thebmc.co.uk/Feature.aspx?id=2628

If you want tickets Come and find me on Monday or Wednesday at the wall or if you can't make it down then call me on 07805799141.
