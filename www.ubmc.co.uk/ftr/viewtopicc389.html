What? A casual University bouldering competition between UBMC, Aston and UCB.

Where? Redpoint Climbing Centre - the cave and the upstairs area.

When? Weds 4th Nov, starting around 1.30pm and ending around 5pm.

Cost? FREE! Standard student entry to the centre with no extra cost for the comp.

Prizes? There will be cash prizes for 1st, 2nd and 3rd in each gender category + Raffle and Spot Prizes

Why should I enter...

Because comps rock! They offer a great chance to shag together with other members of the club in a relaxed and encouraging environment. Whether you've been climbing for years or for less than a month, this casual competition will have something for you. Novices can pick up tips and tricks from the more experienced, and the more experienced can pit themselves against some of Rich '9a' Simpson's fiercest problems. Perhaps most importantly, we can all unite and give Aston a good thrashing!

This proves to be a wicked day guys, the perfect way to get set-up for the Harbourne run in the evening!

BE THERE!
