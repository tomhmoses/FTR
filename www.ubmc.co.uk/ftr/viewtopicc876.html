Dates to save for your diary!
More info on each social to come...

2nd November: Presentation Evening and Curry
A chance to hear about some of the awesome trips members of the club have been on in this country and abroad, followed by a trip to one of the local curry houses

16th November: Team games, quizzes and challenges evening
This is THE not to be missed social of the term. It WILL sell out, have limited team places, so as soon as we start selling tickets get them ASAP!
This evening will be a chance to bond with some members of the club you may not have had the opportunity to get to know yet, plus some good ol' competition and inter-team rivalry! The challenges will be individual, team, difficult, fun, creative and basically awesome!
There will be PRIZES ... and forfeits ...

30th Movember: Movember Party!
For those who have spent the month fashioning their upper lips, this is a chance to celebrate the creations you have styled, grown or pitifully willed.
Those without tash's, do not fear, you will still be involved (men always appreciate moustache admirers!). Get out your fake facial hair, draw some on, and get ready for a good old fashioned party!

7th December: Christmas Dinner
A chance to dress up proper like, (and we mean suited, booted and bootiful) and enjoy a 3 -course sit down posh nosh meal before we all head home for the Christmas season.
