Looking for an excuse for more procrastination? Here's your chance to show off your creative abilities and bag yourself a free t-shirt!

As is tradition, we will once again be having a some attractive t-shirts for the summer trip. This year's rendition is going to be a delightful orange.

Once again, the design of this t-shirt falls on the shoulders of UBMC members. So grab a t-shirt template (or not) and throw out your greatest designs, pictures, wittiest climbing puns and anything you think would look awesome on this years t-shirts.

The committee will vote on the designs and the winner will get a t-shirt for free!

The deadline for entries is Thursday 8th May at midday. You've got one week: get on it!
