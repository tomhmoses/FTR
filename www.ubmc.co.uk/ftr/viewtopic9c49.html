For all of you looking for your next outdoor climbing fix, look no further! The time has finally come for the infamous Low House trip!!!


With spectacular climbs and borderline luxury accommodation (including close proximity so several epic pubs!) this is the perfect excuse to get away from uni work and explore the wonders of the Lake District! A cheeky peak at the 'extremely reliable' 2 week weather forecast suggests we may even get some sun!!

When? - 23rd-25th October. We'll meet at 6pm on Friday outside the back of the Munrow and we'll be back on Sunday evening.

How much? - £35 (£25 for drivers). It may be a tad more expensive than other trips (due to the extra distance) but its definitely worth it!

Sign Ups - Sign ups will be at redpoint (2pm) this Wednesday (14th Oct). There are only 24 places in total so spots will go to the keen beans who get there first! If you're not around for redpoint, never fear, I'll be around at the Guild from 1pm on Wednesday so you can sign up then, or drop me a message on Fb .

What to bring? - Any climbing stuff you may own, warm clothes, good footwear, waterproofs, sleeping bag, head torch, rucksack and food (we will stop at shops on the way).

If you have any questions just give me a shout! If not I'll see you all on Wed for sign ups!
