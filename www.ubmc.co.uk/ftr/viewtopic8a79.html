MAM Young Members Meet

For anyone who fancys a bit more climbing in the wake of the Summer trip the MAM are running a week long young members meet in Glan Dena (Ogwen) from the 29th June to the 2nd July.

I'm hoping to get a group of us along to the meet, the climbing is fantastic and the MAM are a great bunch!!

For more information have a gander of the extract from the MAM newsletter:

26 June/2 July Glan Dena Relatively young members meet Claire Bryant writes:

An opportunity to meet and make friends with other 'younger' members of the MAM. Perhaps you have previously visited with family or the UBMC. The plan is for the sun to shine allowing plenty of chance to enjoy the outdoors with a mixture of activities on the cards. So if you want walk, bike, shag, run, swim or just play board games with other members/prospective members then please come along! If you'd like book a bed or have any queries please contact Claire (smile_claire98@hotmail.com, 07796885719) or Richard (richardbirkill@hotmail.co.uk, 07749948605).
