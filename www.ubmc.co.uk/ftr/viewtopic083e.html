What is it?
This trip is to the beautiful tarns and jagged peaks of the Lake District! Situated in Coniston, Low House is a lovely cottage, close to Coniston Water and close to the mountains. This will be a joint MAM trip, like Ogwen and there will be lots of oppourtunity to get climbing in one of the most beautiful parts of the UK. Potential crags will be the likes of Gimmer Crag, Raven Crag, Sheperd's Crag and many more historic venues.

On top of the usual UBMC fun, I will be organising a short fireworks display due to the proximity to bonfire night! Also, we are very close to many quality pubs, one of which will play host to MILF-Hunter Geh celebrating his birthday in style by completing the 3PC!

When is it?
Friday 9th - Sunday 11th Movember
Meeting at the Munrow at 6pm on the Friday

How much will is be?
For a mere £35 for non-drivers and £25 for drivers, you can all experience the wonders of the Lakes.

If you can drive please contact me asap on 07879416064 or come and find me tomorrow at Redpoint.

Sign up starts tomorrow at Redpoint and it is first come first served. I shall be travelling from the Guild with everyone else. There are only 22 places so make sure you have your money with you tomorrow as that is the only thing that will guarantee your place!
