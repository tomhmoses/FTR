What?: The Annual Dinner. A Formal dinner with a climbing weekend in the Lakes thrown in!

When?: 15th - 17th February meet at the munrow at 6 pm

Where?: The Lake District. We will be staying in a parish hall in Bassenthwaite (with more than 1 shower unlike last time) and the dinner is just outside Ambleside

Why?: Because it is quite possibly the best trip of the year!
