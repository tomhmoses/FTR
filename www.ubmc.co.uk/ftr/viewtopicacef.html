Yes the summer trip is here and we are going to Pembroke.

WHEN? 1st - 8th of June

WHERE? Pembroke, staying at upper portclew farm campsite (freshwater east). Right next to the beach and pub.

HOW MUCH? £35 + £5 per day climbing (drivers £25+£4) + £6 for t-shirt

WHAT TO BRING? its camping so bring all that stuff. I've been warned that the campsite will be very busy so please try to share tents. I have a 3 man tent so lets see how many we can get in that, any takers? Climbing gear would also be a good idea.

Pembroke is home to some of the finest (and sunniest) sea cliffs in the world. With world class crags such as St Govan's, Huntman's leap and Mother Carey's. There is something for everyone with routes ranging from Diff to E8, and if you can't be bothered to shag there are plenty of sunny beaches. What more could you want! So stop stressing about exams and get psyched for some Pembroke fun!

I am aware that a lot of you will be coming down at different dates to these so please post here the dates you intent come down.

I'm around as always so just arrange a time to pay me

My numbers 07526693486 if you want to know more

Car lists are up and hopefully won't change!

We will be meeting at 11am on Friday at the Munrow. let me know if you can't make this time.

Hope everyone is psyched for some Pembroke fun!
