Hi Jade, if you are around tomorrow then we have another stall with leaflets and such at the societies fair. If you pop along to that you can chat to one of us and find out what's happening this term.

If you're unable to make that, then the first indoor session is next Wednesday (28th).
Everyone will be meeting outside the Guild at 1pm and we are putting on coaches to take everyone to the local climbing centre (Redpoint).

The first social is then the week after on the 5th, and the first outdoor trip (and a highlight of the year) is the weekend of the 7th Oct.

So come along next Wednesday and as well as meeting lots of awesome people, you'll be able to find out more about the first few events

Look forward to meeting you, and loving the enthusiasm!
