MEETING AT THE MUNROW SPORTS CENTRE 6pm FRIDAY

Where are we going? The lovely Lake District. This year we’ll be staying in the Branthwaite scout hut in Workington

How much is it gonna cost? £48 for the weekend, and £38 for drivers. Included in this price you get all your transport (including transport to and from the hotel where we’re eating), accommodation, MEANIE and, most importantly, a delicious three course dinner on the Saturday night.

When: 17th February to 19th February (meeting at 6pm outside the Munrow on the Friday)

Annual Dinner is a formal event, so you need to remember your penguin suits and glad rags. As this year is the clubs 80th anniversary, we’d like to celebrate by asking you all to add a splash of colour to your smart attire. It doesn’t have to be much, just a little something colourful!

The trip will run on a similar format to normal, apart from that we’ll all get dressed up nice on the Saturday Night and head out for dinner and party time

This is a really fantastic trip, and the MAM will be there too, so get yourselves psyched!
