ERMAGERDD CERNWERLLLL!!!!!

It's approaching that time of year where the sun makes a guest appearance in this country, and your climbing shoes smell at their worst. So keep revising, and get psyched or Cornwall 2015

Where? Tower Parks Camping, St. Bruyan, Penzance, Cornwall TR19 6BZ. A pretty well kitted out campsite only a £10 min drive from Sennen Cove Beach

When? Official Trip dates are 6th - 13th June. But the dates are flexible. So post the dates you'd like to attend from and to and I'll try and tetris people into transportation.

How Much? £40 (Basically fuel to get there and back) + £6 for each night you stay + £4 for a tshirt.
Drivers get £10 off + a free tshirt.

What do I bring!?
For Cornwall we're getting our camp-on. If you don’t own a tent, team up with friends or other club members - we also have a couple of lovely, intact, pleasant tents in the gear cupboard :/

In addition to camping stuff, you will need:
Sleeping bags
Roll mats
Headtorches
Suncream
Waterproofs
Climbing gear

This years Cornwall tshirts/beaters which this year will be electric blue tie dye with Ray's spectacularly awesome 'Crimp Till You Bleed' design on the back. I'm also hoping to have a day of climbing in 1980s dress (as modelled by J Dawes)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lLyF8zcP674
So get out there and pick your 80s dress!!!!

And if you're not tempted enough, we'll be heading to Up Club & Bar in Penzance so that those of you who still have dignity can lose it

And of course the traditional summer trip BBQ is included in the cost of the trip.
Please sign up below. Post the dates you'd like to stay, whether you're able to drive, whether you'd like a tshirt or a beater and what size tshirt/beater you'd like. If you plan on returning for Gradball (11th June I think) please let me know asap. If you have any questions at all please don't hesitate to get in touch. You'll find me on the end of 07933666415
I am so very very excited!! Much love x

To all those leaving on Saturday 6th June we meet at 9 am outside... you guessed it, the Munrow. So close now I can already taste the mead!
