Right, first week of august is obviously the most expensive time of the year. BUT, http://www.coveholidaypark.co.uk/ are offering caravans from £371 a week, which sleep 6 people. This place is about a 3minute walk from the cuttings, and the boulderfield, two really good crags. Pretty much on the beech.

Unless you can find a campsite around Weymouth I think this is probably the best bet
