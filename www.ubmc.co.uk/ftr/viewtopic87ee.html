Missed climbing trips over the Christmas holidays? Eaten too many mince pies? Keen to experience some sport climbing?? Then look no further, dear Stoat, the Peak Sport trip is the one for you!

When? From the 23rd-25th January (perfect chance to celebrate the end of January exams or to get on a trip before all those horrible deadlines close in...) We'll meet at 6pm on Friday outside the front of the Munrow and we'll be back on Sunday evening.

Where? We will be staying at the lovely Thorpe Farm bunkhouses in Hathersage, (http://www.thorpe-bunk.co.uk/), which will be luxurious compared to the usual UBMC lodgings... with BEDS, SHOWERS, KITCHENS and more...

How much? £30 (£20 for drivers).

Sign Ups: Sign ups will be at Redpoint this Wednesday (14th Jan). There are limited places. so spots will go to the keen beans who get there first! If you're not around for Redpoint, no sweat, I'll be around at the Guild from 1pm on Wednesday so you can sign up then...

What to bring? Any climbing stuff you may own, lots of warm clothes, good footwear, waterproofs, sleeping bag, head torch, rucksack and food (we will stop at shops on the way).

If you have any questions, just drop me an email on secretary@ubmc.co.uk and hopefully see you all on Wednesday for sign-ups!
