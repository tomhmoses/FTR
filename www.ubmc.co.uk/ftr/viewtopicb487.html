May I take this opportunity to revive a beautiful poem of a few years ago, slightly adapted for the new generation. For those of you who are too young, the Proverbial used to be called Varsity

p.s. my choices are already with louise

The crazy cats of UBMC,
Once had their Christmas Dinner at Varsity.
7th of December was the date
The meal at 8.30 and no one was late.
They dressed up beautifully with their best clothes and more,
Suits, dresses... you know the score.
The smell from the kitchen was terribly good
Turkey, stuffing and Christmas pud.
The Stoats all laughed, finding everything funny
With wine on the tables to excite their tummies
Party poppers, hats and crackers galore,
Christmas excitement made the louder ones roar
But then Varsity could satisfy their needs no more
Their plates were empty and their eyes on the door...
Wait a mo! Snobs did someone say?
And they jumped in taxis and went off on their way.
Who knows what happened for the rest of the night,
Perhaps MILF-Hunter found a MILF, or Smithy's mo gave someone a fright.
All I know is they had a wonderful time
For £15 a ticket, CAN YOU HEAR THOSE BELLS CHIME??!
