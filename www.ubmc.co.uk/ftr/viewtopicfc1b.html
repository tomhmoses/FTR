Hey Paul,

I went to Hampi a few years ago, but unfortunately it was before I got into climbing - so I didn't do any there!

I went in the summer and it was really hot - so it's probably best to shag at dawn/ dusk (although this is when the mozzies come out so take lots of DEET!). There may be some bouldering in the shade (e.g. caves), to do in the day.

Hampi is a really beautiful place - loads of temples, and more boulders than I have ever seen in one place before. Hope you have a great time - I'm very jealous!
