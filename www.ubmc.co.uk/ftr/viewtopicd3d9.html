Caving in the Mendips! 2nd - 3rd Feb

A weekend caving in the limestone hills of the Mendips! This trip is suitable for beginners and will be a good, fun introduction to the underground environment!

Times and Dates:
We will depart at 8am on Saturday 2nd Feb and return on Sunday evening

What to bring?:
- Normal weekend away gear (sleeping bag, toothbrush etc)

- Money for dinner @ pub in the evening!

- Food:
Packed lunch for saturday
Breakfast and lunch (quick) on sunday
Cave Snacks! (mars bars or similar energy boost which can survive being squashed!)

- 2 complete changes of clothes (just incase!)

- Cave clothing
Wellies (hiking boots will work here too)
Warm clothing thermals, fleeces (NOT JEANS)
Waterproofs
Headtorch (Petzl Tikka/Myo or equivalent) If you dont have one, let me know and we can hire one from cheddar for a few pounds.

Optional Boiler Suit (Reccomended)- This is hard wearing and will give you and your waterproofs/clothing protection from the rock. They can be bought relatively cheaply from camping/hiking shops
