I would be inclined to tend away from Cotswolds. Like most profit led companies the staff have no clue what they are selling (except one or two). There are other places in birmingham who sell mountaineering kit, Snow and Rock, they have a limited selection but the staff are slightly more competant.
Ideally your best bet is a stop off at somewhere like Joe Brown's on snowdownia, or maybe somewhere in one of the small towns in the lakes.
