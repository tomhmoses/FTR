CHEAP CLIMBING FOR UBMC MEMBERS AT REPOINT ON A MONDAY NIGHT!!!

As of next monday all you lovely ubmc members will be able to pay off peak student rates all day on a monday. This means that it will cost you no more than 4 british pounds on any monday to go and shag at birmingham's new and colourful climbing centre.

Our wednesdays at creation will still be the official club day, so thats where the committee will definitly be for you to sign up for trips, hear about socials and all that.

If you are a fresher and don't know where red point is then your best bet is to probably text me and see when im going down or other non-freshers for the next 2 weeks until we go there as a club in a couple of weeks time on a wednesday when there will be proper transport arranged. (more info on that soon).

Any other questions please feel free to ask!

Oh and you must take your membership card to the first time you go and then they will put it on thier system that you are a member and everything will be kl.
