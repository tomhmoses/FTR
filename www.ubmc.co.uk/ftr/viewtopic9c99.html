Did you miss out on Stanage? Do you want a weekend of climbing in beautiful North Whales? Of course you do!

This year's trip to Ogwen is getting close so sign up ASAP to avoid disappointment! This trip is always popular.

To guarantee your place please pay me as soon as you can. I will be signing up at Redpoint tomorrow. If you cannot make it (and there are still places left) I can meet you on campus on Thursday and Friday.

WHEN will this marvellous event be taking place? Fri. 12th - Sun. 14th of Oct. (Meet at 18:00 outside the Munrow)

WHERE will we be going? HERE The MAM hut by the side of the big lake in the centre of the picture to be precise!

HOW much will I have to pay? The bargain price of £25 (£15 for drivers)

WHAT on Earth do I need to bring?! Here is a short list of the essentials:

  • A rucksack
  • Any climbing gear you already own
  • Warm clothes (lots of thinner layers are better than one thick layer)
  • Waterproofs (incl. trousers if you have them!)
  • A sleeping bag
  • Food for the weekend (we can stop on the way)
  • A headtorch - If you don't own one they are a sound investment
