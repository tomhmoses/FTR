For all of your budding and enthusiastic Stoats coming away this weekend I would like to take this opportunity to tell you some information on two particular bargins to be had at this fine event:
For the Stoats 75th Anniversary the MAM kindly produced a journal containing stories from ex-club members over the 75 years of the club. This paper back book contains over 170 pages of club history at a great price of £5 per copy. This might sound like a sales pitch designed to get you buy them, but im my honest opinion the stories in here are really good, if you think of our trip reports, but make the activities 10x more daring and extraordinary you get a rough idea of whats in them.
Anyway back to the point, we'll be bringing these away with us for you to purchase a copy of this weekend.

Our other additional item making an appearance this weekend is the emerald green stoat embroided tie. Having seen many of the MAM at the anniversary meal wearing theirs from many, many moons ago we decided to produce replicates of these. There is not much more to explain about them really, they are green, have the Stoat logo embroided onto them and will be on sale for £10 this weekend. Some of you may be thinking why are we selling ties for a dinner jacket event, well i dont really know either, but like other club merchandise its worth having because its UBMC material.
Think about it this way, in 20 years time what will you physiclly have to remember your time with us by? Well, if your Michaf C you might still havethe remnants of a cast from a climbing related injury, but anyone else probabilly won't have a lot if anything.

Since we are such nice people we have devised a deal such that you can have both items for £13. Bargin really.

I hope to see lots of you rushing over to me to get this items.
