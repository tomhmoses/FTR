Scotland 2013-14

The month of November is upon us, the BBC is forecasting the snowiest winter in decades, it can only mean one thing…. It’s time to get your psyche on for SCOTLAND!!!!

That’s right, it’s the annual UBMC trip to the Cairngorms- where ice climbing, mixed routes, mountain walking, whiskey tasting, ceilidhs and beards can be found in epic quantities!

The trip is running from the 28th December 2013 – 4th January 2014, so we’ll be there for New Year’s Eve. This means we’ll be dropping in on a local village’s Hogmany celebrations, with fireworks, live music and an entire street of Scottish country dancing!

For those of you who have no winter experience there is a 2 day winter skills course run by Pinnacle of Achievement available, which will give you all the skills you need to use crampons and ice axes in the mountains. This course is compulsory for novices and is subsidised by the club, meaning it is a bargain at £50 instead of £100! There are only 12 places though, so be quick!

The price for the entire week is £150 (plus £50 if you’re on the course). Sign up will start this Wednesday (13th November) at Redpoint so come and find me with a £50 deposit if you want to come! If you have any questions ask away! Places on the trip are limited, so get on it!

A date for your diaries is the 1st December, when we'll be having a navigation training session
10:30am in the Mandela Room in the Guild

We'll also be having a session at the gear cupboard at 3pm on 27th December to fit crampons to your boots and hand out any gear you need. Then we'll go for a curry in Selly around 7pm

GET PSYCHED!!!!!
