WHAT? A lock-in at Redpoint climbing wall : exclusive use of the wall to UBMC members after 10pm.

WHEN? 29th May (Friday) from 6pm till midnight

THEME? Pyjama party! - Bring along any party foods (cocktail sausages, party rings, crisps) and wear pyjamas within reason (no dangly bits or saucy anne summers collections please)

PRIZE? £10 a head, for entry throughout the day and evening (you can turn up at 10am if you so wish)

Redpoint are allowing us to bring any food we may want for dinner and the music will be in the hands of our resident well-known DJ Gadd. It's going to be a party!

It's about a month away, but we're putting this up early as not everyone's exams will be over then, so just to give you some light at the end of the very long dark tunnel

So who's up for it?!
