With creation now changing its boulder problems every month this is a great way to install some fun and light competitiveness into the club.

So here it is the ubmc boulder ladder!!

How it will work-

Every month there are 25 new problems at creation which each member should try. There will be a score sheet telling you which ones these are.

For a first completing it on your first try you will get 10 points

For completing it on your second try you will get 7

For completing it on your third go you will get 3

And if you do it at any point in the month you score 1.

Every month you will pay one or two pounds to enter (still to be decided) and the person who has improved the most will be the winner and will win the cash pot of all the people who entered that month.

First round is January which will be free so everyone has a chance to get a score on the board. New routes go out on Friday so if you go before the first club session make sure you take note of how you do on the routes and keep note of your score.

Your score is based on your attempts throughout the month and doesn’t just have to be on a Wednesday afternoon.

Cheats will be publicly humiliated and owe everyone in the club a pint.
