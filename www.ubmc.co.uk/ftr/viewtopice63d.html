UBMC is doing Movember again and this year, I am your team captain. This year, we will be bigger and better than ever before, raising large sums for good causes. The only way for this to happen, though is for YOU to sign up and grow your "mo" for 1 month!

For those of you that don't know, Movember is a charity that has an event each year. Over the month of N.ovember, participants grow a moustache in order to raise awareness and money for the Movember charity. This charity puts the money towards prostate and testicular cancer research. So it's very important for all you guys (and girls les directly) out there!

To join the team simply go to [url]uk.movember.com[/url], click register, click "I'm new to mo" (or "I'm back to mo" if you did it last year) then enter your details and follow the on screen instructions. Once you have registered you will be able to join our unique UBMC team. Our name is the "UBMC MO BROS"

Girls may also join our cause and raise money as mo sisters, however most of them will not be able to grow a moustache

So please join us in the quest to grow a mo (and raise money for a good cause)! Remember however pitiful you think your mo may be, sign up and who knows, you may receive the most pubescent mo award come the end of Movember at our Movember Party!!!!!

If you don't want to take part then please visit our team page where you can donate to our cause http://uk.movember.com/team/479425

Thanks, and may the mo always be with you
