WHAT: BMC Winter Essentials - Skills and Techniques for the winter mountains
A talk as part of the BMC (British Mountaineering Council) tour, presented by Rob Jarvis (MIC) and Dave Hollinger (Aspirant Guide and MIC). The talk will cover essential skills for exploring the winter mountains, including equipment and clothing, navigation, route planning, avalanche awareness, ice climbing and much more. There is also a free prize draw which is likely to include decent prizes from Lowe Alpine and Asolo.

A group of us went to this last year in Stafford, and found it very helpful and informative. They also show lots of pretty cool photos.

WHEN: Wednesday 21 November @ 1930

WHERE: Guild Council Chambers, the Guild

HOW MUCH: £3 Advance Tickets, available from any Committee member at the wall. Alternatively contact me (PM/Phone) if you can't get to the wall.

MORE INFORMATION: Take a look at the website: http://www.thebmc.co.uk/Feature.aspx?id=2169

This is very much worth going to if you are considering going on our Scotland Winter Mountaineering trip -more information here

Following this I expect we will be having some drinks in Joes...
