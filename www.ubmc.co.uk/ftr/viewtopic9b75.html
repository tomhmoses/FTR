I'd like to know who and how many people are interested in a first aid training course and also when you can attend.

I’m planning an ‘Out-door Activities First Aid Course’ designed especially for our sort of activities. It covers all the basic/standard first aid topics with an emphasis on those relevant to emergency first aid in the out doors and in more remote locations. There will be indoor theory and outdoor practical / scinarion type work. It is a fully certified and recognised award of a high enough standard to be held with SPA, BCU, BELA and such qualifications. It is valid for 3 years - a well worth experience, especially for people serious about climbing.
More info about the course here.

Cost is between £55 and £82 depending on numbers. It could drop to £45 if enough people are interested and this other guy is available. [Also investigating further sponsorship so fingers crossed]. Obviously it gets more expensive with less people so the more the merrier. It would most probably take place in the Guild, 9:00 – 5:00 on two days.

It can either be done in 2 days together (i.e. weekends or during the last week of Easter holls), or on 2 separate days. He is very flexible so…below is a list of dates, please post with when you can attend and I’ll take it from there.

Dates: 19 – 20th April (weekend or just one day)
23, 26 and or 30th April (a combo of Wednesdays and a Saturday)
Or possible any time during week of 21st April
