Signing up for the next UBMC trip has now begun!

New Destination! Gardoms Edge (Directions will be handed out on the day, plus those to The Climbing Works in Sheffield if it is bad weather)

Cost is £7.50. Deadline: Wed 31st October
This is for petrol money, please pay me this (cheques made payable to Katie Marshall) by next Wednesday, thanks. Those who pay first are more likely to secure a space on the trip. You can call round at my house - 16 Tiverton, Selly Oak, to pay.

What a full day's trip to the Peak District, or an indoor wall if the weather is bad
When Saturday 3rd November
Extra costs money for the indoor wall if we use it
Where to meet 8am outside the Munrow sport's centre on Saturday 3rd November
What to bring lots of warm clothes, waterproofs (still handy if a bit damp but ok to shag), any climbing gear you have, lunch, water, money for the drivers and the indoor wall if we use it, and treats to help the drivers do their job
How to sign up post on this thread
Any questions post on this thread, or see me (you can click on my email link below to get my email address)

We will be relying on people's own cars for transport, so spaces are limited to the number of drivers going - thankyou as always drivers!
If you are a driver, please post if you are thanks!

Please may you also post your real name if your username is not your real name!

Hope to see you there!
