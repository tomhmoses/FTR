I was looking at partaking in this years movember when i discovered that you can enter as a team hence i am now on a recruitment drive.

For those of you who dont know movember is a charity that helps raise money for testicular and prostate cancer research through the power of the moustache.

Men who take part (known as Mo bro's) start the month of november clean shaven and are then sponsored to grow the best looking moustache they can for the rest of the month. Styling and grooming are encouraged but complete removal is not allowed!!

Girls (Mo sisters) can also register and get involved with fundraising but you may wanna stay away from the whole hairy upper lip part.

By registering as a team not only will the club look extra cool on trips/socials/etc but together we can raise more money and 'share the growing experience'.
The charity will send people things to help with fundraising (badges, collection boxes, etc) but MOST IMPORTANTLY if we register as a team then they will send us the material so at the end of the month we can host our own MO PARTY!! where we all get together, hand out awards and generally celebrate how awesome (and totally not gay) we all look.

if there is enough interest i will happily organise this!
go to http://uk.movember.com/uk/?home if you you still need convincing
