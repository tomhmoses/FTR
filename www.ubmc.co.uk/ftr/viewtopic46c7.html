And for my final act... The 2015 club hoodies and beaters are now on sale!

Hoodies

We're changing these up a bit this year, and going for something a bit more stylish/wearable than previous editions.

This year's hoodies will be a zipped style, and will be a better quality material than those we've ordered previously. They will be navy, and will feature the UBMC logo embroidered in white on the front left breast, but no design printed on the back - hopefully this will mean they are more wearable than previous editions! See the style of hoody here.

Sizing: S - 36" | M - 40" | L - 44" | XL - 48"

Price: £25

Beaters

Winning Design - Henry Jeffreys

These will also be navy, and will be the nice cotton material we used two years ago (for the red beaters). The UBMC logo will be printed on the front in white, and the winning design will be printed on the back.

Sizing: S - 35-37" | M - 38-40" | L - 41-43" | XL - 44-46"

Price: £10

If you've got any questions at all, feel free to drop me a message.
