Its time to find your competitive edge and start getting psyched for THE CHALLENGE

What is this? We will put you into teams and then on the night it’s up to you to battle it out!
The Challenge will put your mental, physical and other abilities to the test through quizzes and creative challenges! Those epic warriors brave enough to take on these challenges will get the chance to bring victory to their team!

Theme: ANIMATION! So get together with your fellow team mates and pick an animated theme for your team to dress up as. Anything cartoon goes!

Where? TC’s
Interesting (possibly made up) fact: due to UBMC’s tradition of coming here, TC’s was actually named after The Challenge!

When? Weds 6th November
At: 8.00 pm

AND THEN if this wasn’t enough excitement for you… We’ll be heading to… (yeps thats right) SNOBS

Don’t miss out!

Love Emma and Lizzie xxx

P.S please let us know ASAP if you want to come! The sooner we know who to sort into teams, the longer you’ll have to prepare your costume
