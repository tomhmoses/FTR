Couldn't get to Llangollen to sport shag? or did you go and did it give you a taste for more climbing?
Then do we have a great opportunity for you!
The Low House trip is back this term and for all of those who have been before you know how good this trip is.

When: Friday 11th- Sunday 13th
Where: Low House, Coniston
How much: £15 for drivers, £25 for non

This is a great chance to get outdoors and do some climbing in the beautiful Lake District and after that slightly longer drive to the lake you will be treated to a warm bed for the night, fully equipped kitchen and greeted by a crackling wood fire in the traditional living area. What more could you want? Cookies please


OOOOOO YEAH!
