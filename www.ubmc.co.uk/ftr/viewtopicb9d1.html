The UNMISSABLE final trip of the term, UBMC's day trip to THE ROACHES!

Never forgiven yourself for not getting that V4 last year? Super psyched to try outdoor bouldering for the first time? Tired of trad faff and want to get some serious crushing done? Then come bouldering with us!

Alternatively...

Fancy some more trad on legendary peak district gritstone? Haven't quite got your trad fix yet this term? Been longing to go on a trip without the cold, cold nights?! Then come (roped) climbing with us!

THE DAY TRIP HAS IT ALL!

Don't worry if you've never been on a trip before or never bouldered outside before, there's bouldering problems and routes going from complete beginner to completely impossible so it's bound to be a fun day for everyone, plus, it's only a single Saturday!

When? - Saturday the 5th December. Meet at the Munrow sports centre at 7:30am.

How much? - Â£10 (free for drivers). The cheapest trip of the year!

Sign Ups? - TONIGHT at 8pm. There'll be a link to a form to sign up on the facebook page and here.

What to bring? - Any climbing stuff you own, warm clothes, lunch and a water bottle!

If you have any questions then hit up either me or Brain! Hopefully see you on the trip!
