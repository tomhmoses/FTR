Everyone apart from twigger and Professor Sensei Chris we are meeting at 6pm on friday at the munrow.

It looks like it will be cold so bring warm clothes, waterproofs.

You will also need a sleeping bag, plus lunch for sat and sun, plus sat night, we can stop for food, (milk etc) on friday.
Any problems let me know.





After an amazing annual dinner, keep your brains in trad mode for a weekend of Welsh climbing.

What- A weekend of fine Welshs trad climbing, staying in the luxurious Glan dena hut.
When- 5th-7th, leaving 6pm on friday 5th, back sunday evening.
Where- The amazing Glan dena hut, Ogwen.
How much- £25 (£15 for drivers) if you can bring money on wednesday it would be great.
How- Sign up with me at redpoint (wed 24th) from 2pm, or ring me from 5pm- 07833681234


Ok,

First off, this is the last trip of our current committee

Second, for those of you who haven't been to Ogwen before, you have missed out, from single pitch to multi pitch there really is a mix for everyone, and for those who haven't stayed in the hut before, you are in for a treat.

Thirdly, This is a joint trip with the MAM, so you all get a chance to pick the brains of some truely experienced climbers.

All you need is- warm clothes, sleeping bag, climbing gear and lots of enthusiasm.

Lets send off this committee the right way.
