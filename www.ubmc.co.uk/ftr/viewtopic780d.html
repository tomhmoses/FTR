Hi Guys once again its time for the ABSOLUTELY BRILLIANT Ogwen trip.

WHEN? Friday 16th - Sunday 18th March (mother's day)

WHERE? The glorious Ogwen valley in North Whales, staying at the MAM's Glan Dena hut

HOW MUCH? £25 (£15 drivers)

WHAT TO BRING? Any climbing stuff you may own, warm clothes, good footwear, waterproofs, sleeping bag, head torch, food (can stop on the way), Lots of Enthusiasm!

This is the last trip of the semester and the first trip the new committee will be running, so come along to see them in action. As I always say North Whales is the mecca of rock climbing in the UK, with something for everyone, no matter what your climbing ability. The Ogwen trip was probably the most 'memorable' trip of last semester, and has been the scene of many UBMC 'adventures', so sack off mother's day (yes you know your priorities) and join the fun in Ogwen!

The last trip was very popular so to avoid disappointment please pay me as soon as possible. I will start sign up at Redpoint on Wednesday, if you can't make it then I'm on campus everyday.

My numbers 07526693486 if you want to know more
