Trip number 4 is next up, and its a good one! We're off to the Yorkshire Dales, home of the famous Almscliff crag. The trip will also adopt a learn-to-trad-lead theme, for those that want to give trad leading a go! For those that don't it will still be a great opportunity to shag in Gritstone heavens like Almscliff, Ilkley and Brimham.

When:
Friday 26th - Sunday 28th of February.
Meet at outside the Munrow Sports Centre at 6pm on Friday 26th.

Where:
We’ll be heading to Pateley Bridge in the Yorkshire Dales, The accommodation is a Scout Hut, but a rather nice warm one with a kitchen and good showers.

How much?
The price of this trip is £30 (£20 for drivers). Drivers get an additional fuel allowance of £80 which they are encouraged to use, and keep the remainder to help contribute to their vehicles wear and tear

How do I sign up?
**Sign ups will be on Thursday 18th of February at 6pm**
Sign ups will be through a google docs form which will be released on the Facebook event page.

Once you’ve signed up with your details you will need to pay in the following week to secure your place.

What to bring:
Lots of warm clothes - thermals, fleeces, down jackets, hats, gloves, buffs etc
Waterproofs (Jackets and trousers)
Comfortable shoes to walk to the crag in
Roll mat (if you have one bring it along!)
Food for 2x Breakfast, 2x Lunch and Dinner (although there will be the opportunity to stop for food along the journey up)
Warm sleeping bag
Headtorch
Any climbing equipment you own - shoes, helmets, harness etc (although you can borrow a club helmet + harness if you don’t have any yet!)

Much love,
Sensei Chris x
