Take a break from revision and get psyched for the summer trip - we’re going to Pembroke, home to some fantastic sea cliff climbing across a whole range of grades!

WHERE: We’ll be staying just outside of Pembroke, at Upper Portclew Farm Campsite: the finest, cheapest campsite in the area! It’s a great location, just a five minute walk from the beach and there’s a pub nearby. The postcode for the campsite is SA71 5LA.

WHEN: 6th - 13th June. Unless otherwise specified in the car list, we'll be meeting around the back of the Munrow (near the gear cupboard) at 11am on Friday.

HOW MUCH: £40 + £5 per night (£30 + £4 for drivers). This includes a BBQ that we'll have during the week! T-Shirts are an additional £5.

WHAT TO BRING: We’ll be camping, so make sure you have a tent and everything else that goes along with it. If you don’t have one, team up with friends or other club members - we also have a couple of tents in the gear cupboard.

In addition to camping stuff, you will need:
Sleeping bags
Roll mats
Headtorches
Suncream
Waterproofs
Any climbing gear you own
Unprecedented levels of psyche!

N.B. If you’re coming to Paddles, leave your dignity at home.

Important Notes
GRADBALL: I realize many of you will want to go back for Gradball on the 12th June. If you’re going back for that, please let me know as early as possible.

T-SHIRTS: Once again there will be t-shirts for the trip - I’ll be putting up a design competition thread alongside shortly. The colour is yet to be finalised, but will be posted soon.

PADDLES: If you aren't tempted by the quality climbing and sunny beaches on offer, then surely you won't be able to resist the temptations of Paddles - a club which has attained legendary status within the club for its levels of scattiness. We'll be heading down there on the Saturday night.

I’m hugely excited to go back to Pembroke, and hope to see a lot of Stoats coming! Post below which dates you would like to come for. If you have any questions at all or need to contact me, my number is 07803577235.

Thanks!

Sam
