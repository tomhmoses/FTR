Lots of classic Joe Brown routes. That man is a legend. (classics at Cromlech and Cloggy!)
Get on the slate.

Not be on a trip where someone ends up in hospital.

A few routes I have been losing sleep over:

Bouldering - Simple Simon, Clever Beaver, Jerry's Roof, Left Wall Traverse, Rock Atrocity, Not To Be Taken Away, Ziggy's traverse, Font, Jerry's Traverse(cratcliffe), Starley's Overhang.

Trad - 'The Corner', Cemetery Gates, Left Wall, Strapadictomy, Fern Hill, The Crank, Saul's Crack, Regent Street, The File, Lots more Cracks, some Slate.

Cracks - Beyond the Azimuth, Charas, Brown's Crack

Sport - Hot Fun Closing, Rubicon, Nomad, Axle Attack


New aims: Kitten Claws, Zeppelin, Space Cadet, The Pit Problem Low, Bus Stop, Pill Box Original, Demon Wall Roof, Captain Hook, Young American, The Press, Kudos, Kudos SS,


Good times:
Dorset trip, from start to finish, climbing, company, the weather and badgers with necklaces in the square and compass. So good.
Pembroke - So much climbing. Epic-ness of Space Cadet, mega steep.
Evening soloing @ Stanage with Byron on the first good weather day after exams.
Parisella's cave - All of the people who go there are legends.
Trowbarrow and Bowderstone - Best ticklist I've assembled on a trip!
