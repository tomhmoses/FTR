That’s right – it’s the return of ‘The Challenge!’ The event where we put you into teams and get you to compete against one another in a quiz all about climbing, Halloween and the club itself! Along the way there will be practical challenges, in which you have the chance to be a hero and bring glory to your team, usually by doing something ridiculously brilliant!

WHEN: Wednesday the 31st of October, hence the ‘Halloween Challenge.’

WHERE: The event will be held in Subside club, which is a stone’s throw from our favourite haunt – Snobs! We’ve been given the top floor to ourselves for the evening, and we’ll have access to the bar there too.

To make life nice and easy, we’ll be meeting in Joe’s Bar at The Guild for some pre-drinks at 7:30 PM, before making our way to Subside for 8.30 PM.

Naturally this shindig will end in Snobs, which is just across the road and well within walking distance!

DRESS CODE: HALLOWEEN OF COURSE! The only rule is that once you have been sorted into teams, your team must all agree on an outfit, and you must all wear the same outfit. The more outlandish the better, as there will be PRIZES! So get creative!

IF YOU’RE IN FOR SOME FUN THEN LET ROBIN OR I KNOW ASAP SO WE CAN SORT YOU INTO TEAMS!! After all, you’re going to need the time to get sorting out your outfits!

Big love,

Heather and Robin
