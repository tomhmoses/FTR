It's time for THE BEST TRIP OF THE YEAR!!!!!!

It's back ... Our big trip to STANGE in the Peak District.



You've met our committee, now it's time to meet MEANIE, MING AND SOCK WRESTLING. For those of you new to this wonderful league of extraordinary rock lovers, MEANIE is a magical concoction concocted with love and happiness and boooooooze (the secret recipe to which is handed through the generations of social secs). You'll have to wait and see what ming and sock wrestling are ...

WHO: Aaaaanyone who has joined UBMC!! No experience needed!! You have to pay the £35 otherwise we won't have you covered on insurance To become ONE OF US click this link https://www.guildofstudents.com/student ... aineering/ (you'll need to log in on my.bham guild stuff

WHEN: FRIDAY 7th-9th October We'll meet outside Munrow at 6pm!!!

WHERE: We're staying at Thorpe Farm Bunkhouses in Hathersage in the Peak District.

HOW MUCH: £40 for non-drivers, £30 for drivers (don't worry, the other trips aren't this expensive)

SIGN UP FORMS ARE GOING TO BE POSTED ON FACEBOOK AND EMAILED OUT ON FRIDAY AT 6:30pm Message Bobbie or Jack to sort out actually paying!! We'll be at Boulder Central on Monday and Redpoint on Wednesday!

WHAT TO BRING:
Your fine self
Sun lotion (don’t forget your sombrero)
Warm clothing (enough for 3 days) - fleeces, hoodies, thermals, hat, scarves, gloves etc. - Even if the sun is shining down on us it can get quite chilly!
Waterproofs - We do live in Britain after all.
Walking boots or comfortable shoes to walk to the crag
Cozy pyjamas
Toiletries + towel
Sleeping bag
Roll mat (if you have one - there are beds)
Headtorch
Personal first aid kit
Food
Spare $$$ (no weather guarantees - might have to visit an indoor wall or cafe)
Cooookieees
Inflatable UNICORNS
SUNSHINE

Unless you have them already, we'll supply harnesses and helmets! Bring shoes if you have them, but no worries if you don't have any!!

Social looove,

Jobbie
