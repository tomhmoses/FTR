In case I haven't annoyed you enough with questions already, let me make myself clear: VERY keeen on the summer trip

A combination of mountaineering and climbing sounds awesome, I'd also like to do the course I've been hearing about. As far as dates are concerned, anything beginning late June/early July would work best for me, will probably be in Germany until then.

And just to put it out there, although I'm sure there will be loads of fancier options on the table: Romania (aka Gypsyland, as I know some of you affectionately call it, although there's rarely any at those altitudes lol) is always a very affordable option for sport climbing/mountaineering. There are some cool outdoor festivals going on in July/August giving the REALLY keen ones the chance to compete in quite epic trail running or mountain biking marathons and ultramarathons. You usually set base camp on festival grounds (in the heart of the Carpathians), participate in the activities they have organised or go do your own thing during the day and in the evening you can go to gigs or hang around camp fires, etc. It's a very cool experience, the sceneries you get are quite dramatic and you usually find people from all over Europe at this kind of things. I'm personally going to prioritize other options over this one just to break the habit, but if anyone's interested - especially if you're looking for something low-budget - it's definitely worth it and I can most certainly help.
