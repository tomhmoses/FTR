GUESS WHAT'S AROUND THAT CORNER!?!!!
It's everyones favourite social: TEAM CHALLENGES

Who: Everyone new or old !! Once everyone has clicked attending on the Facebook page we will put you into your teams! Don't worry if you don't know people in your team - you soon will!!!

What: A madhouse of stupid and silly challenges and a quiz to get you in the competitive mood

Where: Meet at Joes at 8pm for drinks then we’ll venture upstairs to Beorma bar for some epic challenges and then to the magical home of many a drunk stoat: SNOBS!!!!

When: Joes at 8pm, Beorma at 9pm and then taxis to snobs at 11:30ish to boogie our little butts off

Why: Why wouldn't you want to put your face in a plate of flour?

Theme: Anyone from the world of FILM and TV. Each group will choose a film/tv show to dress up as!

We hope you can make it and look forward to seeing your ridiculous costumes!!
