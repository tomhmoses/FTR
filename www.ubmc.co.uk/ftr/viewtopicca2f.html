What? Perfect gritstone bouldering, followed by dinner in Hathersage

When? Saturday 6th December, leaving early morning and returning the same evening

Where? The Burbage South Circuit, Peak District

Cost? £7 for non-drivers and £4 for drivers

'The Burbage South boulders provide one of the friendliest circuits in the Peak.
There are stacks of easy problems, all with perfect landings, and the occasional
harder problem tucked in between them. Pleasant and low, in a lovely setting,
it really is a great place to start a bouldering career.'

Peak District: Bouldering

Plans For Saturday Morning! Meeting @ Munrow Sports Centre 6.30am prompt! If you're not there by 6.45am we're gone.

What Do I Need? Rock boots and plenty of warm gear

Gareth's Warm Gear Will Be...

    Hat
    Gloves
    Warm Jacket
    Water Proof Jacket
    Hoody
    Tshirts x 2
    Boots or Snug Trainers

IT IS GOING TO BE BALLFREEZING. PACK NO LESS THAN THE ABOVE! Oh, and some trousers...

We'll be stopping at Morrisons to rendezvous and buy food on the way. See you on Saturday my bouldering amigos!
