Yorkshire Climbing Trip: 18th-20th Jan 2008

What: A fantastic weekend trip to Skipton in Yorkshire. A range of grit climbing and limestone routes for the more talented in the beautiful northern countryside.

When: 18th – 20 Jan. 18:00 Departure from Munrow Centre, expected return late Sunday night.

How: Staying in a self catered scout hut with a short drive to the crag each morning.

Cost: £21 (£11 for drivers). Please pay asap. Deadline for payment is wednesday 16th Jan.

Sign Up?: The trip is now full but please do post here if you would like to come in case people drop out.

What Do I Need To Bring:

-Waterproofs
-Warm Clothes
-Rucksack
- Climbing gear
(let me know on the Friday if you need anything in particular – harness, belay plate etc.)
-Sleeping Bag / roll mat
- Head Torch
-Food for 2 breakfasts, 2 lunches and 1 dinner (will stop on the way for food)
