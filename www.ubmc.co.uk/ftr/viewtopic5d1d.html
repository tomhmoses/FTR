Hello all,

What?:Club trip to Ogwen to experience more north Whales trad!!
Where?:Staying at the Glan Dena Hut as per usual
When?:22nd - 24th October
How Much?: £25 per person, £15 for drivers, £5 if you can prove to me that you are a mushroom
What will you need?: Climbing Gear, Sleeping bag, warm/waterproof clothing, and food for saturday night

We currently have 26 places to fill, possibly more if we overflow like last time.

Drivers wanted! Post below if you are interested in driving
Thats all, my numbers 07933666415 if you would like more info!
