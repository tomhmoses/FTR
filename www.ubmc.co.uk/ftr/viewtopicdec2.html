IT's time to decide what fine ale we shall be sipping from the luxury of our fine hut during the Scotland trip. The taste notes are outlined below (hint: Wildcat=value)

Stag

4.1% ABV

Mahogany coloured with medium to

light body. Challenger and Fuggles

hops are added plentifully giving initial

bitterness which is then balanced by a

caramel finish from the roast malts.

Trade Winds

4.3% ABV

Light golden in colour, with a high

proportion of wheat giving a clean,

fresh taste. The mash blends together

with Perle hops and elderflower

providing a bouquet of fruit and

citrus flavours.

Cairngorm Gold

4.5% ABV

A golden coloured, light bodied

continental style beer. Saaz hops

provide an initial fresh and zesty

flavour with Styrian Goldings providing

late aromas.

Nessie’s Monster Mash

4.1% ABV

Predominently malty but well hopped

giving a satisfying fullness of flavour

with an enjoyable lasting finish. A

glass or two is a must before an

audience with the monster!

Black Gold

4.4% ABV

Multi-award winning beer. A Scottish

stout with a wonderful rich dark

colour. Smooth full bodied with subtle

bitterness giving way to late sweetness

and underlying roast barley hints.

Sheepshaggers Gold

4.5% ABV

The best beer baa none!

An exceptional continental style

beer with good body and refreshing

aftertaste.

Wildcat

5.1% ABV

Deep amber in colour with a complex

malt and fruit flavour and a delicate

bitterness from Challenger and Fuggles

hops. Strong & distinctive.

Choose wisely!
