Next term the UBMC are going to be producing a journal of last year and this year so far.

It will consist of the trip reports and selected writings from UBMC members. There will also be a photos section. It's a great way of remembering your time in the UBMC so far, the people you've met, the places you've been and the nights you don't remember...

If anybody has any pictures or wants to write an article about anything at all to do with the UBMC and have it published in the journal email it to ubmcjournal@hotmail.co.uk

This journal has been going for many years so it would be great to produce a good one for past, present and future members to read!

We look forward to hearing your stories and have a very merry Christmas.

Love from,

the Committee
