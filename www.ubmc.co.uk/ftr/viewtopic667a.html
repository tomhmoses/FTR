LUBE or Llangollen? Llangollen everytime! For the beginner, Trevor Rocks is a perfect place to learn the basics. Then for the adventurous World's End has some awesome exposed trad climbing. And in between those 2 crags you have 6 other crags offering sport from F4-F8b and trad from diff-E7. So whether your a beginner or experienced, there is loads to go at on this trip! It's all about being outside. One downside may be, that it'll be bloody freezing!

( For the record I think Llandudno is way better but there isn't much beginner stuff there! )
