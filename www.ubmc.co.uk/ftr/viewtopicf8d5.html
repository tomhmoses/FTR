I WILL BE MAKING THE DRAW TOMORROW


IGHT PEOPLE,

I hope everyone is having a good Easter and getting some work, revision and climbing done.

I'm going to be hosting Round 2 of the UBMC bouldering competition at Creation on 27/04/09. It's going to be carried out in exactly the same fashion as the first competition. It is also going to be cheaper! I will only be charging £2 entry into the competition, coupled with the £3 creation charge means it will only cost £5 overall! That's cheaper than a nights entry to the wall, and you also have a chance to win some more FANTASTIC prizes!

Also, for everyone who registered a score for the first competition you have the opportunity to win the overall most improved. So make sure your climbing lots over the next few weeks to improve your chances of this prize!!

Prizes are as follows:

    Best Male : £10 to spend in the creation shop
    Best Female : £10 to spend in the creation shop
    Overall most improved : £20 to spend in the creation shop
    Random Prize Draw : A Fingerboard!!! (RRP £70)


Unfortunately the routes for this competition are currently up, so if you could avoid bouldering at creation for the next week or so, it make the competition fairer to everyone!!

See you all soon,
