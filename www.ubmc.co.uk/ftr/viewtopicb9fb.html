Thats it. After being thoroughly discouraged and upset by the high flying claims, aims and achievements of the modern UBMC, I despair and create this topic for all in the same sad position.

The girls are young, fit, good-looking, skinny and talented and way better climbers than I could have even dreamed of being.

The boys young, fit, mostly hot, strong and all thrashing 6cs and E1s all over the shop.

UBMC has entered a new (or returned to an old) era of fantastic climbing determination, dedication and talent. And now, as an official Old Git (finally) I am sad to admit that we (you know who you are, generation 06/07) can no longer call ourselves UBMC greats. The 90's kids have stolen the crown and taken over the rat. Feed him well children, feed him well.

I wholeheartedly disagree Jules.

Whilst it is true that the climbing being done at the moment is impressive in its ambition and achievement, I still consider our year(s) some of the best the club has seen. The focus is changing, but I think what our committees achieved was immense, and let's face it, laid the way for the revolution that is occurring now. If you have any doubt, have a look again at some of the trip reports and photos from the years we were on committee.

Don't despair, but feel proud that we played our part in the continuing story of UBMC and Feed the Rat.
