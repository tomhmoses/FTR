This it it guys!!

The British University Bouldering Comp is a 4 round competition in which each uni's top three scores will be taken.
Each round will consist of 25 seperate problems. You will get 10 points for doing a route the first time, 7 the 2nd time and 2 the 3rd. There will also be bonus holds on each of the routes which you will get 1 point for if you reach the hold but then fail to reach the end. As uni sport goes this is very laid back and will be a fun day of competion as well as a chance to pit your self against other uni's.

For more info look up
http://www.bubc.org.uk
