ChrisW, Laura and I are going to the Costa Blanca in Easter for a lovely sunny climbing trip and we were wondering if anyone else wants to come to bring it up to an even 4. There's all grades to go at with thousands of single pitch sport routes, long multipitch trad routes (13 pitch severe anyone?) single pitch trad and multi pitch sport, there's even some bouldering! Basically everything on rock at roadside venues. We want to go from Sat 22- Sat 29 March and if we book before 15th jan we can get 30% off flights-normally about £120-140 return inc taxes and charges. Accomodation will probs be £150-£200 for an appartment where we get a proper bed each (no sofa beds). So no more than £50 each for the week. Car hire is usually around £90 for the week for a compact but i did find one company for £60. Everything out there is cheap.

Please post soon if you're interested.

Update: Now going 24th-31st of March one proper bed still available (plus sofa bed). Flights are still £120
