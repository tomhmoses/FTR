Hello!
This is what the club gets up to:
1 epic freshers trip to the peaks where around 50 of us will go shag and then have a massive party!
After that there is a trip roughly every other week, mostly trad but if you will find plenty of members who love sport.
Every other week we have a big social, last year these involved bar crawls, army fancy dress laser quest, stars in their eyes and a big xmas dinner.
Lastly we do a week long winter mountaineering week in the cairngorms which the uni help pay for!
This club is one of the biggest and most active in the university and the best thing I have been involved in at uni.
If nothing else come and find us at the societies fair on thursday/friday in freshers week, look forward to seeing you!
