Did you miss out on the amazing Stanage last weekend? Do you want a spot of climbing in beautiful North Whales? Well look no further!!

This year's trip to Ogwen is getting close so sign up ASAP to avoid disappointment! This trip is always popular.

To guarantee your place please pay me as soon as you can. I will be signing up at Redpoint tomorrow. If you cannot make it (and there are still places left) I can meet you on campus on Friday.

WHEN will this marvellous event be taking place? Fri. 25th - Sun. 27th of Oct. Meet at the Munrow at 6pm on Friday

WHERE will we be going? Glan Dena MAM Hut in Ogwen (http://www.themam.co.uk/index.php/huts-mainmenu-35/glan-dena-hut-mainmenu-38)

HOW much will I have to pay? The bargain price of £30 (20 for drivers)
