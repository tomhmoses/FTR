Yorkshire Climbing Trip: 7th-9th November 2008

What: This time we'll be off to Skipton in Yorkshire for an awesome weekend of climbing (and clubbing for the hardcore).

When: 7th-9th November. We'll be meeting at 6pm at the Munrow Sports Centre, and we will be returning late on the sunday evening.

Where: Staying in a Scout hut in Skipton, unfortunately this one dosen't have beds so bring something to sleep on (if you have anything) such as a roll mat as well as a sleeping bag. There'll be games like football/cricket in the hall on the evenings as well!

Cost: £25 (£15 for drivers). Please pay as soon as possible. The deadline for payment is Wednesday 5th November. Money can be given at Redpoint today or at Creation/social next Wednesday (5th November) or ring me on 07951153862, or pop round to 212 Heeley Road

Sign Up?: This afternoon at Redpoint from half 1 -- look for me! If there are spaces left then sign up will continue from 7pm on this thread

What Do I Need To Bring?:
Waterproofs
Rucksack
Climbing gear (let me know on the Friday if you need anything in particular – harness, belay plate etc.)
Sleeping bag
Something to sleep on like a roll mat (if you have anything)
Head torch (if you have one)
Food for 2 breakfasts, 2 lunches and dinner for friday and saturday night (or eat before we meet on the friday)
Warm clothes
