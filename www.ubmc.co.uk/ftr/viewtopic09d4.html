Josh Smith wrote:

Well... the question you should be asking is.. "To look like a douche, or to not look like a douche?"

Do your trousers have built in gaiters? - If so, then your sorted. Gaiters a) Sweat up your legs like no tomorrow b) make your calfs and ankles more baggy than trousers on their own = more likley to catch your crampons on them. c) make you look like a complete Wayfairy.

As a mountain guide in the Alps once told us: Stay Cool, Be Cool, Look Cool. - You certainly don't want to overheat, sweat then proceed to chill down to ridiculous levels. You need to have a cool head about you when climbing, panicking certainly doesnt help matters. And of course, if you can hit the first two points, you will definitley look cool. Funky colours make you easier to be spotted as well as adding a bit of "style" lol

Gaiters make you overheat + are likely to make you trip and get flustered = you won't look cool.
