Hi Guys its once again time for another Low house trip

WHEN? Friday 8th- Sunday 10th of March

WHERE? The glorious Lake District, in the town of Coniston, staying at the MAM's lovely Low House hut

HOW MUCH? £40 (£30 drivers)

WHAT TO BRING? Any climbing stuff you may own, warm clothes, good footwear, waterproofs, sleeping bag, head torch, rucksack, food (can stop on the way), Lots of Enthusiasm!

For those of you who got their first taste of multi-pitch climbing at the weekend, there is plenty more in the lakes. There's plenty of top quality climbs, of all grades, all in a beautiful setting. Low house is usually a very chilled trip, and lots of climbing gets done. This will also be the first trip run by the new committee, so come along to see them in action.

There are limited places so pay me asap to confirm your place. I will be starting sign up at Redpoint tomorrow.

My numbers 07526693486 if you want to know more
