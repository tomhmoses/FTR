Hope everyone has their *Compulsory* fancy dress sorted out! Pop into Harborne Wednesday or Thursday! Failing that, a few cars will be going to charity shops Saturday morning. Has to be done in the name of tradition


For those of you who are not acquainted with our trip the stunning Yorkshire town of Skipton, be prepared to have an awesome time! On the weekend of the 26th-28th of October, UBMC will be heading up to the land of farmers, crackin' accents even more crackin' crack climbs and classy ladies (Ask MILF-Hunter and LARD-HUNTER)

Oh yes, its the trip where we pull out all the stops. In terms of climbing, you may have the fortune to visit Almscliffe Crag, the Cow and Calf at Ilkley or even Brimham rocks. Saturday night will involve our annual fancy dress (see below) visit to to the club STRATA, the place to go in this dynamic hub that is Skipton. Our accommodation for two nights will be the affectionately named scout hut called "The Dump" - (That's its real name!) which is in close proximity to Strata itself!

Fancy Dress - This year's fancy dress will be charity shop themed. Basically, if you're off with a friend, you need to pair up and buy an outfit (Top and trousers/skirt) for them. There are only 2 conditions:

1) It must be from a charity shop
2) It must be as horrific as possible (As long as they will be able to get into Strata)

If you're off by yourself, I can pair you up at redpoint or you can buy your own terrible outfit!

THE PLAN
Meet at the Munrow at 6pm on Friday the 26th October
Get sorted into cars and arrive at 9-9:30
Tick off all of the classic routes at the crag on Saturday
If you don't have a fancy dress sorted - potential trip to the charity shops on the way back
STRATA!!!
Some more awesome routes ticked off on Sunday

COST
It's going to be £30 for non drivers and £20 for drivers. Please let me know ASAP if you can drive as we need as many as possible. My number is 07935451113. Give me a bell if you have any problems. Signup starts TOMORROW at Redpoint. I will be there from about half 2 onwards as I have teaching till 2.

KIT LIST
WARM AND WATERPROOF LAYERS
SLEEPING BAG
A CAMPING MAT AS WE'RE SLEEPING ON A FLOOR
CLIMBING GEAR
SOMETHING TO WEAR TO STRATA
HEAD TORCH
WASH KIT
FOOD (Will be stopping off to buy food so not essential)

Hope you're all as excited as I am!

maltloaf (Secretary)
