WHAT: The UBMC Harborne Run 2007, probably the best pub crawl you'll go on all year!

WHEN: We will be meeting at different places at 7:30pm sharp on Wednesday 7th November. There will be Team Selly Oak meeting at the Goose, Team Pritchatts Park meeting outside the reception at Pritchatts House and Team Vale meeting outside the reception at Shackleton and then everyone will be heading to Harborne.

WHERE: The main event will be starting at The Green Man at around 8:00pm when everyone show up

WHY: Because it will be fantastic and you don't want to miss out, it'll be one of the best nights you may or may not remember this year!

THEME: Everyone going will have to buy a white T-Shirt with mine and Rosie's design beforehand for Â£3.50, perfect for drawing all over in felt tip pens!

We'll be going to a number of places in Harborne finishing off at Varsity, come along whether you drink or not, there'll be some entertaining ways of getting from each pub to the next. The T-shirts will be on sale at the climbing wall in the afternoon and beforehand if you can't make it in the afternoon. Some of us will be heading to Risa afterwards. Who wants to come along for this one?!
