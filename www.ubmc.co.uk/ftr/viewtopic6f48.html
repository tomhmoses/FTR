Errm. THE outdoor show!

Its at the NEC this Friday, Saturday and Sunday

It is for climbers, kayakers, Mountian Bikers, Hikers, etc. It hosts the European Boldering competion and normally has some climbing master class things loads of outdoor stuff for sale, you can try out stuff, buy far too much stuff (i bought a kayak last year on a spur of the moment kind of thing) There is normally a pool for kayakers to play in, stuff for kids to do (normally some artifical cave) Normally a ice climbing wall and this year it also has an amazing hard core new climbing challenge - although i think its for professionals only - ohh and biking arennas - some rowing or kayaking thing out on the lake and stuff everywhere

Anyway - go to www.theoutdoorsshow.co.uk for more information - it tends to be a great day out if your not doing anything though
