The much anticipated ANNUAL DINNER is upon us (well, almost!)

For those of you that dont know, this is THE trip of the year! From ball gowns and dinner jacks to holed rock boots and soggy chalk bags...this trip has it all!

What?: An epically eventful weekend in Ogwen
When?: 25th-27th February
Where?: We will be staying at Eric Jones huts in Tremadog (Not too far from Ogwen)
How much?: £50 (£40 for drivers) - This includes ALL of your transport to/from Vales, 3 Course sit down meal, evening entertainment, travel to/from the meal venue AND MEANIE!

Travelling on the Friday evening as normal, we will shag on the Saturday, go out for a slap-up meal and a partaaay on the Saturday night and then attempt to shag again on the Sunday (Hangover dependent).
This is always a great trip, with an excellent turn out from MAM members too!

SIGN UP WILL START THIS WEDNESDAY (9th FEB) - FIRST COME (with money) FIRST SERVED!

NOT TO BE MISSED! - Set this as your homepage, Write it on your forehead....Do whatever you have to not to miss this trip!

The menu is as follows (Please decide your options and let us know when you sign up!)

Starters:
- Oven baked goats' cheese wrapped in Parma ham with cranberry sauce
- Chicken liver pate with salad leaves and melba toast
- Stuffed Peppers, with a Tomato & Basil Sauce (v + VEGAN!)

Mains:
- Braised Welsh lamb shank with potato puree garlic and redcurrant sauce
- Salmon wrapped in filo pastry with seafood sauce
- Home Made Vegetable Wellington served with a Stilton Sauce (v)
- Roasted Half Pineapple filled with Stir Fried Vegetables served with Rice (VEGAN!)

Desserts:
- Sticky toffee pudding with cream
- Fresh fruit pavlova
- Sherry trifle

Tea or Coffee
