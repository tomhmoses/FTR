Hello all!

Now that the fleeces are done for the year, it's time to move on to hoodies and beaters. We have yet to decide a colour for the hoodies, but the beaters are to be a fetching shade of purple akin to the committee tops for 2013/14, just as they were red last year to match that committee's tops.

As is always the case it is up to UBMC members to design the logos that appear on the hoodies and the beaters. Pretty much anything goes so long as it is related to climbing and not too offensive.

As an added incentive, the winner of the design contest gets their hoody/beater/potentially both for free as a reward.

Post all your design submissions below and the committee will decide on the winner. You have until the 9th of March. Hoodies and beaters will be on sale at the start of next term, just in time for the arrival of loan and in anticipation of the summer trip.

Big love,
