The last trip of the year is here, the very exciting xmas bouldering trip...

A day of bouldering in the peak district, followed by dinner in a pub...that's pretty much all there is to it, boulderings a really sociable laid back way to shag so it's loads of fun whether you're a hardcore boulderer with a beanie or have never done it before!


When: Saturday 8th December, 8am
Where: Outside the Munrow
How Much: £8 + dinner (pay at the pub)
What Do I Need: Some warm clothes, climbing boots if you've got them and enough food to keep you going until dinner
How Do I Sign Up: Find me (James) at Creation, or give me a call on 07786247625
