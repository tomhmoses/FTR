    Sleeping bag
    Waterproofs
    Warm clothes
    Plenty of layers
    Headtorch (opt)
    Walking boots (opt)
    Food for 1 dinner / 2 breakfast / 2 lunches; or
    Money for the above. We'll stop at a supermarket en route
    Any climbing gear you own
