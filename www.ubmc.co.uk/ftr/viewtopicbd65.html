Anyone interested in seeing what a bouldering competition is really like??

This is a friendly competition between us and Aston, so just for fun and not part of any league. The idea of it is to be a fun and different way to spend a Wednesday afternoon! If you took part in the Warwick friendly last year, it will be the same sort of thing, except that it's in Birmingham, so there's no need to rely on drivers!

Date Wed 27th Feb
Start time 2pm
Cost Normal student entry price - £3.50
Venue The Redpoint Centre (The new climbing centre in Birmingham)
What to bring Climbing shoes, chalk if you have it, money, water and a pen - very useful for scoring!

I will arrange getting there nearer the time and once I know how to get there! I will probably post directions and a meeting point with someone who knows where they are going.

Post here if you'd like to go!
