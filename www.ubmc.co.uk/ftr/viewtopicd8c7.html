BMC Alpine Lecture 2012 in Birmingham!

The BMC Alpine lectures are back for 2012, with the excellent line up of Nick Bullock (leading alpinist) and Tim Neill (Mountain Guide) presenting.

Sponsored by Berghaus these lectures will make you aware of the skills required to tackle the alpine environment safely.

The partnership of Nick Bullock and Tim Neill is sure to inspire any budding alpinists out there! Topics covered include:

best areas to visit
glacier travel
weather
huts & bivvys
avalanche awareness
route selection
and the dark art of moving together.

There will also be a Berghaus free prize draw!

When: 26th April. 19:30 - 21.30
Where: LR6 In the Arts Building

I am going to be giving tickets away FREE to club members on the 25th April at RedPoint and the Bournbrooke Wall BBQ in the evening (Or drop me a text if you can't make it).

Tickets are also available for £5 for members of the BMC or £7 for non members on the door on the night.

This lecture will be very useful for anyone keen to do alpine mountaineering this summer! not to be missed!
