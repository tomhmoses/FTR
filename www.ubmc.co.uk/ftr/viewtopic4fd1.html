Right, you may have heard me mention this a few times, but this is the official plan for the UBMC Charity Everest shag March 17th 2010

What it involves is a team of about 7 climbing a wall in redpoint as many times as it takes to reach the summit of Mount Everest. Impossible, you say?

This was done two years ago by 5 members of the club in just 8 hours raising £500. Four of us were freshers and one of the group had literally started climbing a few months beforehand so it is possible!

This year I am hoping for 3 teams of 7 which should mean £2100 based on the last time! The money will be going to Mountain rescue/Air ambulances - exact details will follow soon.

How it will work: Each team will have one route, every member will do a set of 10 climbs and then keep rotating until all the climbs are completed. Everest is 8848m, Redpoint is 11.5m so each team has 770 climbs or 110 each!

I have a list of names but do come find me if you want to do this!

Thought I would put this off the initial post otherwise it may have been information overload!

A DEGREE OF FITNESS WILL BE REQUIRED!!!!

However with just under a month that is plenty of time to get fit enough. Things that we did to get fit:

Sets of 10: What you will be doing on the day so it is a good idea to do some of these!
Sets of 5: However these have a twist as you have to down shag each time! Trust me it gets hard!
Bouldering: Pick 5 fairly easy problems and do them one after the other with no rest in between. Have a short break and do it again.
Running: It can't hurt doing a bit of CV! We used to run to creation before we would train
Pull ups and dead hangs: Good to do when you cant get to the wall

For a really good training program chat to Byron our resident fitness geek
