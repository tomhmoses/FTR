Hi Matt, good to hear from you.

We all go climbing in Mosely (http://www.creationwall.co.uk) on Wednesday afternoons where we get student rates. If you come along people from the club will be there from about 1:30 until whenever we're too tired to shag any more. If you grab one of the committee members (wearing a blue polo shirt) they'll be happy to chat with you about what we do and where we go etc.

If you're having trouble finding a way to get there put a post on here and hopefully someone will able to give you a lift or share a taxi. It doesn't take too long to walk either.

There's more information on http://www.ubmc.co.uk

Hopefully we'll see you there.
