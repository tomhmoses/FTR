UBMC presents, the penultimate trip of term, a day full of wonder with guaranteed fun for all who attend:

The Trad Day Trip to Froggatt Edge! - Sign Up below!

Next Saturday (19th November) we're heading to the Peak District once again for a day full of trad climbing, sun and general warmth! (maybe not those last two...)

It's the perfect opportunity to get back out on the rock one last time before those end-of-term deadlines become too daunting, and we only require your commitment for ONE DAY! Everyone can spare a single day for climbing right?! Whether you drank too much at Stanage to experience grit stone in all it's glory or you're just keen for your first experience on real rock the trad day trip can cater to all!

Where: Froggatt Edge, "The main edge is a virtually continuous face of exceptionally clean and quick-drying rock of the highest quality" - UKC/Rockfax. It's near-ish Stanage and Hathersage and has a huge variety of routes spanning most styles and grades! The weather is pretty good currently but there might be a last minute switch if things turn bad. UKC page: http://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crag.php?id=22

When: Saturday 19th November, 7:30am! It's an early start so we can make the most of the day once we get there, meeting in the Munrow Sports Centre car park!

Sign-Ups: https://goo.gl/forms/LZ4bN3Uepvtix7Cj2 Go go go now!

What do I need? Warm Clothes - Bring everything you own if necessary, it certainly won't be warm (Sorry, I lied earlier.) Lunch + Water - We won't be stopping on the way to pick up food so please bring whatever you need to keep going for the day!
