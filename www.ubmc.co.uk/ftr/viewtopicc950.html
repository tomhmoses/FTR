This year I would like to...:
    ~Break my no UK sport climbing rule and visit the Diamond (Will not be till September though!) and possibly Anstey's. Great year at Anstey's. Climbed The Cider Soak which is an amazing route and flashed Empire of the Sun. Both of which are mega classics and I hope more people get to go down there and experience them.
    ~Another day of trad would be good if it was some Peak Grit or Pembroke Lime. Didn't get on any Grit trad. I did manage a couple of trips to Pembroke. Ticked off some mega classics E3's and 4's and even managed a couple of E5's. Finally got Bloody Sunday which was literally the best fun I've ever had on a trad route. Please if you are climbing E3, just give it a go. It's got jugs and gear all the way up barring the crux. Amazing.
    ~Boulder in Font - just need to book the gite and ferry! I ended up going twice. You can never get enough Font. Can't wait to go back. An unbelievably quick and cheap holiday.
    ~Decide whether to Boulder in SA or Sport in Thailand... Decisions. Didn't got to either. Got roped into a couples holiday to the Dalmation Coastline instead. Let's face it, holidays are shit unless there is some kind of climbing or skiing involved.
    ~Go back to Rodellar (Oct half term or xmas holidays)x I went to Benidorm instead. Flashed a couple of 7b+'s. Great climbing and recommend it to anyone climbing 7a-8a. Forada is one of the best sport crags I've been too. v
