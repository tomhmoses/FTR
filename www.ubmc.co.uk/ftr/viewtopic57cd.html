Believe it or not the time has come again for what has to be one of the BEST socials of the year...The Harborne run. I hope you're all psyched...because we certainly are!

For those of you that aren't veterens of the club, the Harborne Run is a very special social. We will be taking you on a magical tour of all the best pubs in Harborne...beginning at the Green Man and finishing off at the Proverbial before heading onto snobs. This year Louise and I have decided to theme the social 'Where's Wally?' so we will be selling t-shirts in RED and WHITE (yay!). We expect to see you all sporting sexy specs and ready to hunt out your favourite drinks!

So what's the crack?

When: Wednesday 19th October

Where: We'll be meeting at the Gunbarrells at 7.30 and grabbing taxi's over to Harbourne (or if you prefer, you can meet us at the Green Man at 8pm)

From there we will be heading to:
Harbourne Stores
The White Horse
The Proverbial
