It's a little late this year, but as is tradition here is the aims thread for 2012!

This is the place to record all of your dreams and aspirations for the coming year, whether they're climbing or none climbing related.

Perhaps like Byron you want to tick an 8A or like Neil you want to solve a cryptic cross word, and not kill anyone this year, or maybe like Sam you just want to make it onto the Israeli bobsleigh team.

Here goes.

I want to do more:
Sport routes than in 2007
Alpine routes than in 2007
Trad routes than in 2008
Winter routes than in 2010
Boulder problems than in 2011

Dyno my way onto the top ascents page of ukc (so probably boulder 7C),
Get in shape for font which is 8 weeks away. (and so shag at least 3 Font 7's there)
Stop finding potential new routes and actually shag some of them.
Be able to do 10 pull ups in a row.
Buy new solutions.
