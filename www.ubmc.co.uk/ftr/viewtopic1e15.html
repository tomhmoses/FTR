The AGM will be held on Wednesday 10th of March in the Learning Centre LG32 from 6pm.
It's really important that as many of you as possible come to this. It is where you will decide who runs the club next year.

Candidates will need to be nominated and seconded by members of the club. There will be a round of speeches on why you would be the best person for that position and a round of questions will follow. Speeches for the non-executive roles don't have to be too long at all so don't let that put you off.

Once this has finished candidates will leave the room. The vote will then take place. ALL VOTES WILL BE ANONYMOUS.

UBMC Committee Positions

President
(Executive Position)
The president has overall responsibility for the club, he or she chairs committee meetings,
supports the rest of the committee and generally oversees everything that goes on.
Unfortunately, tradition requires that no females are allowed to run for this position.
Talk to: Gareth

Vice-President
(Executive Position)
Works closely with the president in the role of generally looking after everything
and acts asdeputy to the president should he or she not be available for any reason.
The VP is also responsible for running Pembroke/Cornwall, the clubs health & safety and training courses.
Talk to: Michaf C

Social Secs (2 positions)
Social secs organise our fortnightly socials which can be (more or less) anything they choose,
although a good balance between alcoholic and more chilled out socials is always a good way
to go. They are also responsible for running Stanage and the Annual Dinner.
This is one of the easiest roles on Committee - because there are two people it hardly seems like they do any work at all.

Mountain Sec
Runs the Scotland trip and organises any other winter climbing related events that they want to bring in.
Must have killed at least one full-sized Polar or Grizzly bear.

Indoor Sec
