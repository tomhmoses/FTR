Right guys,

At Creation this Monday (9th) we're holding a bouldering competition starting at 6.30PM. This competition is for EVERYONE and will follow the same format as Jack's comp which has ran in previous weeks. I want as many people at the wall as possible.

Prizes are as follows:

    Highest scoring male = Free entry to Dave Macleod's lecture
    Highest scoring female = Free entry to Dave Macleod's lecture
    Overall most improved = (Will be awarded at next competition)

    Random prize draw = Free 3 hour Coaching session with the man himself!

Entry cost of the competition will be Â£3. We are currently speaking to Creation to try and arrange a reduced entry fee for the evening. For those who cannot shag on Monday night the deadline for entry and score submission will be 5 o'clock on Wednesday evening. This means that people who are unable to make it on Monday can still enter and shag their problems on the time in between. Scores and the winner of the random prize draw will be announced on this thread on Wednesday night.

To try and measure interest could people please reply to this thread to say whether you're going to be coming?

I'LL SAY THIS ONCE MORE. ANYONE CAN ENTER. ANYONE CAN WIN, LET'S START BOULDERING PEOPLE. If you're finding a problem hard just ask for help. This isn't meant to be super competitive, i've learnt that these comps are ridiculously laid back, so just give it a go.
