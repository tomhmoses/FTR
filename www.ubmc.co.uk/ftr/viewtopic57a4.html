Moving to Australia for a year unfortunately means that I am going to have to bid farewell to the love of my life . Here's a few details:
- Perodua Kenari GXi (Yes, it's a GXi not just a standard model!)
- X reg (2001)
- Taxed until September 2011, MOT'd until December 2011
- 989cc, meaning extremely cheap to insure!
- 85,000 miles on the clock
- 3 uncareful owners resulting in a dent in every panel of the car (none of which were done by me)
- manual gearbox
- 4 seater (back seats which recline individually)
- Averages 35 - 40 mpg
- CD player, immobiliser, alarm, cigarette lighter and fog lights (all the possible extras you can get on a Kenari)
- The roof rack and boot fit bouldering mats perfectly, and the seats fold almost flat, perfect for sleeping in!
- Terminal velocity 85mph
- Unbelievable amounts of headroom

I drove one of these all the way from London to the capital of Mongolia last summer. 8,600 miles in 26 days over some of the shittest roads in the world and arrived having only 1 puncture and slightly broken rear suspension. What this car doesn't have in looks it makes up for in reliability!

Let me know if you're interested either on here or text/ring me on 07760252248. Maybe even the club could buy it as a club car
