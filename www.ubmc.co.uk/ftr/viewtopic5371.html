You've heard the rumours, you speculated for at least a week and a half but here it finally is! The biggest trip of the entire year,
ANNUAL DINNER!

So what's the big deal? Why should I, having never been on this trip before, sign up?
Annual Dinner is the biggest trip of the year, topping even Stanage! Current stoats, old gits and even members from the Midlands Association of Mountaineering (MAM) will descend on north Whales for a weekend of climbing, merrymaking and a swanky 3 course meal!
The climbing features some excellent multipitching (just be sure not to accidentally end up on the E1 or E3 finish to Christmas Curry!) and a hut that is a whole 10 meters from the crag.

Donde esta la crag?
We'll be staying near Tremadog in the famed Eric's Bunkhouse complete with showers, a (very small) kitchen and Eric's Climber Cafe across the road! http://www.ericjones-tremadog.co.uk/

When are we heading to Tremadog?
Grovel and apologise to your significant other, or just drag them along for a romantic weekend! The date of this trip is February 13-15.
We'll be meeting at the Munrow sports centre at 6pm on February the 13th and getting minibuses!

How much dollah?
For a 3 course meal, wine, meanie, DJ, accommodation and transport there and back the trip will cost you £60 (£50 for drivers)

What's happening on the Saturday evening??
Like all good nights it will begin with suiting up! Bring your silkiest dresses and smartest suites, we dress to impress! The classiness of the evening only continues with MEANIE! We will then continue on to the Royal Goat Hotel where we'll enjoy our three course meal and wine! Speeches and prizes will litter the evening and once we're done eating we will have a DJ to allow us to dance the night away!

How can I sign up?
We'll be signing people up and collecting dollah on Wednesday 28th January at the Guild and at Redpoint.
Bring your money and menu choices (find the menu below) with you otherwise we wont be able to confirm your place! Places will sell out quickly so get on it to avoid disappointment! If you can't make it on Wednesday then give your money to someone who will be and they'll be able to pay on your behalf.

What shiny gear of mine should I bring?
Smart clothes for the Saturday night
A WARM SLEEPING BAG. It will be cold so get your toastiest sleeping bag!
A roll mat if you have one
A stove or something to cook with - the kitchen is very limited
Thermals
Fleeces
Down jackets
Waterproof jackets and clothing
Approach shoes
Any climbing kit you own
Gloves
Head torch


The Menu!!

Starters
Tomato and Roast Red Pepper Soup
Smoked Haddock and Y Fenni Cheese Fishcakes with Leek Marmalade
Ham Hock Terrine with a tomato and apple Chutney

Main Courses
Slow braised Minted Welsh Lamb with Leeks and Crunchy Bread
Baked Cod Loin with a Y Fenni cheese crust and cream sauce
Baby Corn and Spring Onion Thai Curry with Coconut and Jasmine Infused Rice
Honey Roast Ham Salad

Dessert
Dark Chocolate and Peppermint Terrine with fruit coulis
Steamed Lemon Curd Pudding with custard
Selection of luxury Ice Creams
