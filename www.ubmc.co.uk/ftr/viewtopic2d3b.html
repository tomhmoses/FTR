The official club indoor climbing days for 2009-10 are as follows:

Wednesday Afternoon (1pm Guild): Repoint Climbing Centre (£3.50)

Monday Evening (6pm): Creation Climbing Wall (£3.50)

The Committee has worked super hard to secure these great deals for UBMC this year and would kindly ask that so far as possible people really make the effort to head to the 'official' wall on the 'official' day.

Many of you may be aware that Creation, for a limited time, are offering student entry on Wednesday afternoons at £1. Let me state that UBMC as a club will not be availing of this aggressive price cutting. Having worked very closely with Redpoint to ensure a very successful Freshers introduction and a great first term package we will not be jumping ship for the sake of a few quid.

Having said this, the decision as to where you shag and when you shag is ultimately your own. Please just bear in mind that UBMC values your company. With two official days at two different walls there is enough variety to keep everybody happy.

Hopefully by now everybody knows the deal with Wednesdays at Redpoint. Monday nights at Creation will officially be starting in just under two weeks time on the 26th October. Further information regards transport and meeting times will be made available in the coming week.

Let me just finish by saying that with two great walls both offering exceptional student deals the UBMC has never been in a better position to turn out enthusiastic, sociable and talented climbers. It would be a great shame if, instead of using these opportunities to our advantage, a situation were to develop whereby one half of the club does not know the other because they shag in different places at different times. Keep it in the family homies.

Gareth C.
