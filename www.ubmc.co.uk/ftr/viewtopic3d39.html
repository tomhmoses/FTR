Ladies and Gentlemen, it is time to announce the next trip for you lucky people! We are going to SKIPTON!

For anyone who hasn't been before, it is one of the best trips of the year! Skipton is fantastically placed, being close to loads of superb gritstone and limestone crags. It also features a pretty banging nightlife, which Hallie has kindly agreed to give us a tour of on the saturday night!

What?: Two days climbing on the beautiful yorkshire crags, and one hell of a saturday night at STRATA, Skiptons finest club!
Where?: Skipton in beautiful Yorkshire
When?: 19-21 November, leaving on friday 6pm and returning sunday evening
How Much?: £25, £15 for drivers. This includes entry to strata! If you do not want to go to strata then let me know and I will reduce the price.
What will you need?: Climbing Gear, Sleeping bag, warm/waterproof clothing, and PSYCHE for the saturday night!
