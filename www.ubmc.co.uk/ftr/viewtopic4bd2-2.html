This traditional annual trip has a little bit for everyone - climbing, eating, DRINKING and laughter!! Climbing both Saturday and Sunday in the beautiful Lake District, with a black tie (yes that means dressing up!) meal on the Saturday evening at a very very nice venue!!

Get your gladrags on and join us plus ex-members in the celebrations!!
