Time for some elevated, quick drying grit bouldering!

Yes, climbers, it's time for more bouldering. This time at the beginner-friendly Birchen Edge. There is a huge amount of easy climbing to be done here, but for the beasts there's still quite a few hard climbs around!

When? - 7th of February. We'll meet at the Munrow at 7:30am.

How much? - Â£10 (free for drivers). Cheapest trip of the year!

Sign Ups - Sign ups will be at Redpoint this coming Wednesday (28th Jan). The amount of spots isn't fixed yet, as it depends on getting more drivers, but there's at least 15 spots open at the moment! If you want to drive, or have a pad, please let me know either when signing up or before hand!

What to bring? - Any bouldering stuff you may own, especially climbing shoes, a chalk bag if you have one, lunch and a bottle of water.

If you have any questions just give me a shout! If not I'll see you all on Wednesday for sign ups!
