Word of warning Michaf C, it's not exactly the most clear guidebook in the world. Distinct lack of pictures and full of descriptions like, "the second cleft right of the deep cleft round the corner" and "start to the right of the grassy slope" (not much use on a grassy hillside!). So make sure you photocopy enough pages to find where the hell the routes are! So many wasted hours. (Find a mate like I did who has the book and also already knows where bits are!)
