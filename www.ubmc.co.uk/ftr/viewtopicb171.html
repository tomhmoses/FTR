WHAT: The Christmas Dinner with your favourite club!

WHERE: Varsity in Harborne, you might have been sober enough to remember it on the Harborne Run

WHEN: Wednesday 5th December, at 7.30pm

THEME: Formal dress code for this one, suits and dresses everyone!

WHO: Everyone, and bring girlfriends/boyfriends along as well for this one if you want, we'll be able to have anywhere up to 80 people there

COST:£10 to be paid to me or Rosie by the 29th November, come to 4 Rose Cottages or 48 Harrow Road to pay the money, or you can pay at the munrow on monday evenings or at creation on wednesday afternoon. If you want a place, you will have to pay by the deadline.

Dinner will be served at 8.00pm and for the £10 you'll get a 2 course meal of turkey and extras and christmas pudding w/ brandy sauce, or there are vegetarian options of Red Pepper Risotto, Vegetable Lasagne, Blue Cheese Canaloni or Lentil and Aubergine Dish. If you want one of the vegetarian options please say so and which one you'll want. You'll also get a bottle of sparkling wine between every 3 people, vouchers you can use on the night including a free shot of corky's and 2 for 1 offers and the usual crackers, hats and antlers. There will also be a raffle going on for £1 a ticket with bottles of wine and beers up for grabs and some of us will be going to oceana afterwards so get yourself a ticket for it if you want to come along. Everyone should come along, it will be awesome!
