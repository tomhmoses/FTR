No UBMC summer trip is complete without all members uniformly decked out in a hideously coloured t-shirt, adorned with some kind of witty and creative design.

As is standard, the job of coming up with said witty and creative design rests on the shoulders of UBMC members themselves. Grab a t-shirt template (or don't, if you're feeling some first world anarchy) and throw out your greatest designs, pictures, wittiest climbing puns and anything you think would look awesome on this years lime green t-shirts. A genuine challenge, because the shirts will be pretty hideous.

The committee will vote on the entries and the winner will get their tee for free. Win.

You have one week, so until Saturday the 4th of May. GO GO GO GO!!
